About Walt Disney

The Walt Disney Company, commonly known as Disney, is an American multinational, mass media and entertainment conglomerate that is headquartered at the Walt Disney Studios complex in Burbank, California.

Ariel

Ariel is a fictional character in Walt Disney Pictures' 28th animated feature film The Little Mermaid. Ariel is voiced by Jodi Benson in all official animated appearances and merchandise.

Belle

Belle is a fictional character in Disney's 30th animated feature film Beauty and the Beast. Voiced by actress and singer Paige O'Hara, Belle, the book-loving daughter of an eccentric inventor, yearns to abandon her predictable village life in return for adventure.

Tiana

Tiana is a fictional character in Walt Disney Pictures' 49th animated feature film The Princess and the Frog. Created by writers and directors Ron Clements and John Musker and animated by Mark Henn, Tiana, as an adult, is voiced by Anika Noni Rose, while Elizabeth M. Dampier voices the character as a child.
